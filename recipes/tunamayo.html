Tuna Mayo

Link back home

Ingredients

  • 1 x tin of tuna
  • 2 x Spoons greek yogurt
  • 2 x Spoons mayonnaise
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Open tuna and drain liquid
  2. Tuna in a bowl
  3. Add mayo and yogurt and mix together
  4. Season with lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste
